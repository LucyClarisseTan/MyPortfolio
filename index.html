WELCOME TO LUCY'S PORTFOLIO!

Hello there and welcome to my side of the internet. My name is Lucy Clarisse Quieta Tan. I'm your local digital artist and aspiring animator. I hope to create meaningful and memorable pieces for people of all ages, and bring to life whatever they envision. Thank you for visiting my profile!


GET TO KNOW ME!

My love for art and design stems from my interest in various forms of digital content and online media. I enjoy playing video games, watching cartoons and animations, and most importantly, drawing digitally and traditionally. If you find yourself interested in the style of my work, do not hesitate to contact me!


I'M A DIGITAL ARTIST!

Art is a form of language and self-expression, and it is important that my works are able to convey and capture the concepts and experiences that people wish to share with others. I believe that art is a life long journey, so I'm always looking for ways to develop and improve the quality of my pieces.
